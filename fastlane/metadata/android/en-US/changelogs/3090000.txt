What's new in v3.9:

• Password-protect your backups — encrypted exports use AES-256 with a password only you know
• Import prompts for the password automatically when you pick an encrypted backup
• Wrong passwords let you retry without re-picking the file; your existing data stays untouched
• A one-time reminder explains that lost passwords can't be recovered — pick something you'll remember

Plain JSON backups still work the same.
